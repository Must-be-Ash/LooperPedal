FAQ

What instruments can I create?

At first, there will be piano, bass, drum and guitar but we plan to add more.

How does SoundLoad work?

SoundLoad uses advanced AI to convert your vocal input (singing, humming, or whistling) into various instrument sounds. It's like having a full band at your fingertips!

Do I need musical experience?

Not at all! If you can hum a tune, you can use SoundLoad. It's designed for both beginners and experienced musicians.

When will SoundLoad be available?

We're currently in development and plan to launch our beta soon. Sign up for early access to be the first to know.
